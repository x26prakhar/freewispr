freewispr

Free speech-to-text for Windows.
No cloud. No subscription. No data leaves your PC.

Download for Windows
open source runs locally Windows 10 / 11 powered by Whisper AI

What it does

Everything you need to
speak instead of type.

Dictation

Hold a hotkey, speak, release. Your words appear at your cursor in any app, any text field.

Meeting Transcription

Records your mic and system audio during calls. Transcribes everyone in real time with timestamps. Saved to your PC automatically.

Fully Local

Whisper AI runs entirely on your machine. Your voice never touches a server. Ever.

Setup

Up and running
in under a minute.

1

Download the .exe

One file, no installer. Just download and run — freewispr needs no system-level setup.

2

Run it

Double-click freewispr.exe. On first launch it downloads the ~150 MB Whisper AI model once, then sits quietly in your system tray.

3

Hold Ctrl+Space to speak

Hold the hotkey, say what you want to type, release. Done. Change the hotkey any time from tray settings.
